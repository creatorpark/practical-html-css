Pre vs Pre-wrap

New Lines

Original Text
Multiple     spaces collapse.
New lines are preserved and may wrap or not.
pre - preserve
Multiple spaces collapse. New lines are preserved and do not wrap.
pre-wrap - wrap
Multiple spaces collapse. New lines are preserved and text wraps.

Spaces and Tabs

Original Text
This is a    tabbed   and    spaced    text example.
pre - preserve
This is a tabbed and spaced text example.
pre-wrap - wrap
This is a tabbed and spaced text example.

Text Wrapping

Original Text
This text will wrap when it reaches the edge of the container.
pre - no wrap
This text will wrap when it reaches the edge of the container.
pre-wrap - wrap
This text will wrap when it reaches the edge of the container.

End-of-line spaces

Original Text
spaces at the end of the line
pre - preserve
spaces at the end of the line
pre-wrap - hang
spaces at the end of the line

End-of-line other space separators

Original Text
Text with - 
separators at the end of the line
pre - no wrap
Text with - separators at the end of the line
pre-wrap - hang
Text with - separators at the end of the line
